About Us

Ahtar Oo Co., Ltd was initially established back in 2009 as a trading company, mainly specialized in trading and distribution business. Today, with a better understanding of the importance of security and the flow of information, the company transitioned into providing IT-based home and office security solutions to the masses.

The company’s core competencies are not limited to a strong ability in sourcing, well versed in the IT sector, understanding of the country’s trading procedures and policies, proper channels for export and import and implementing our products.

We put our customers’ needs and customers’ stratifications ahead of ourselves while maintaining a level of credibility and originality. This enabled the company to excel in an increasingly competitive landscape.
