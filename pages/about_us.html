About Us

Ahtar Oo Co., Ltd was initially established back in 2009 as a trading company, mainly specialized in trading and distribution business. Today, with better understanding of the importance of security and the flow of information, the company transitioned into providing IT based home and office security solutions to the masses.

The company’s core competencies include the following but not limited to a strong ability in sourcing, well versed in the IT sector, understanding of the country’s trading procedures and policies, proper channels for export and import and implementing our products.

We put our customers’ needs and customers’ stratifications ahead of ourselves while maintaining a level of credibility and originality. This enabled the company to excel in the increasing competitive landscape.

Our Mission Statement

Ahtar Oo company is dedicated to building a long-term relationship with customers through quality products & services and wants to be recognized as a leading company in providing solutions in each related field. The company’s philosophy is to grow at a steady rate while sharing the successes with the employees and the customers alike.

Our Vision Statement

Strike to win one customer at a time for life, with quality focus mindset and customer first philosophy
